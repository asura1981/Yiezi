About Yiezi

Yiezi is a massive multi-user online role-play map.

You can use Yiez by web browser and Facebook account as Virtual Reality in the real world.

Yiezi lets you place an avatar in google street view/google map, and see others' avatars. In the visible area, the movement of avatars will be presented by animation.

By Yiezi, you can search address and move your avatar to where supported by google street view. And you can also see your Facebook friends who are using Yiez, send instant messages to them, move to their avatars, or broadcast messages to others whose avatars around yours.

You can go multi-user online sightseeing, social communication, manpower guiding, geography and traffic information exchanging by Yiezi.

Before using Yiezi, see also Environment Setting and Operation Manual for Yiezi.
